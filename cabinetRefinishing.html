CABINET REFINISHING

Is your kitchen in need of a facelift? We specialize in cabinet refinishing & painting.

Extensive Prep Work

We take the time to do everything the right way to ensure a perfect job. We start by:

  • Completely disassembling all cabinets and hardware
  • Set up doors and drawers
  • Tape off all counter tops, floors, appliances, etc
  • Sand all cabinet surfaces
  • Apply primer
  • Sand once again with fine sandpaper
  • Caulk and patch all seams

Attention To Detail

After all that prep work is done, we apply two coats of premium paint. Then we add an additional two coats of protective clear coat. Upon completion we reset all the hardware and doors and adjust them so that they fit properly. Any imperfections are touched up and new door stoppers are added to each door. Our crew will clean up the surrounding area and leave your kitchen looking spotless. We guarantee that you will be satisfied with the results.

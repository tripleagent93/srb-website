EXTERIOR PAINTING

Painting is the most impactful way to increase the value of your home and protect your investment. We take great pride in our work and strive to meet all of your expectations. We’ll arrive at your home on time and begin covering plants, bushes and decks. Our process begins with:

  • Pressure washing
  • Scraping loose paint
  • Spot priming
  • Caulking/patching
  • Protect/mask off all windows, concrete and roof

And then we will apply two coats of Premium Kelly Moore paint on all walls and trim surfaces and clean up the surrounding area
